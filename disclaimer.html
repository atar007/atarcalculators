Disclaimer

ATAR estimates, not official results.

This site helps you plan. It does not replace your official ATAR. Here is exactly what that means.

Last updated: June 2026
The short version.
  • Every number here is an estimate, not your real ATAR.
  • Only your state's admissions centre can give your official ATAR.
  • We are independent. We are not linked to any admissions centre or university.
  • Course cutoffs are a guide and change every year.
  • We show ads to keep the site free. We do not control which ads you see.

1. These are estimates

This site gives ATAR estimates. They are not your official ATAR. Only your state's admissions centre can give that. We build our estimates from public scaling reports. Real scaling changes each year. So your true ATAR may differ from ours.

2. We are independent

We are an independent website. We are not linked to UAC, VTAC, QTAC, TISC, SATAC, the SACE Board, UTAS, or the BSSS. We are not linked to any university. We use their public data, with thanks. We do not speak for them.

3. Accuracy

We work hard to keep our data correct. We update it when new reports come out. Even so, we cannot promise it is error-free. Use our results as a guide, not a final answer. Always check official sources before you decide.

4. Not professional advice

This site does not give career, legal, or financial advice. Our content is for general information only. For advice on your future, talk to your school or a careers adviser.

5. University cutoffs

Course cutoffs on this site are a guide. They change every year. Bonus points and pathways can change what you need. Always check the university's own site for the latest cutoff.

6. Advertising

This site is free to use. We pay for it with ads. Ad networks may use cookies to show ads. We do not control which ads you see. Seeing an ad is not a sign we back that product.

7. Links to other sites

We link to other sites, like universities and admissions centres. We do not control those sites. We are not responsible for their content. A link is not an endorsement.

8. Use at your own risk

You use this site at your own risk. We are not liable for any loss from using it. This includes choices you make based on our estimates. Always confirm your results with official sources.

Questions about this disclaimer? Email us at hello@atarcalculators.com.

This site provides estimates only. Your official ATAR is issued by your state admissions centre — for example UAC (NSW/ACT) or VTAC (VIC) — and takes precedence over any estimate here.

9. How we keep estimates honest

We take accuracy seriously even though these are estimates. Every calculator is built on the official scaling report for that state, we show a confidence range rather than a single false-precise number, and we publish exactly how each estimate is produced on our methodology page. When a new scaling report is released we update the data and note it in our changelog, and we never adjust numbers to make a result look better than it is.

10. Your data and your choices

Because every calculator runs in your browser, we do not collect or store the marks you enter — our privacy policy explains what is and is not collected, and our terms of use cover how the site may be used. If you ever spot something that looks wrong, our contact page tells you how to report it, and the about page explains who we are and why the site is free.

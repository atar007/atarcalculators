Privacy policy

Your privacy matters.

Plain English, no legal traps. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your information.

Last updated: February 2026
The 30-second summary.
  • Your marks never leave your browser. We don't see them, store them, or share them.
  • We use standard website analytics (page views, device type) — no personal data tied to you.
  • We show ads. Ad networks use cookies. You can opt out.
  • We don't sell your data. We don't even collect anything we could sell.
  • If you email us, we keep that email so we can reply. That's it.

1. Who we are

This privacy policy applies to ATARCalculators.com, an independent website offering free ATAR estimators for Australian Year 12 students. In this policy, "we," "us," and "our" refer to the operators of ATARCalculators.com.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

If you contact us by email (e.g. atarcalculators@gmail.com), we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The contents of your message
  • Any attachments you choose to send

We keep this only as long as needed to handle your inquiry and any follow-up.

Information collected automatically

Like virtually every website, we collect some basic technical information when you visit:

  • Standard server logs: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring page, pages visited, time of visit
  • Analytics data: aggregated, anonymised stats about how the site is used (e.g. "1,200 visitors viewed the NSW calculator this week")
  • Cookies: see section 4 below

What we do NOT collect

The most important paragraph in this policy:

When you use any of our ATAR calculators, the marks you type stay entirely in your browser. We never send them to a server, never log them, never see them. They aren't tied to your IP, your visit, or anything else. You can verify this with your browser's developer tools — there is no network request when you press "Calculate."

3. How we use information

We use the limited information we do collect to:

  • Operate and improve the website (e.g. fix broken pages, speed things up)
  • Understand which calculators and articles are most useful
  • Respond to emails you send us
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or technical issues
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone — ever
  • Build a personal profile of you for marketing
  • Share your information with universities, schools, or tutoring companies

4. Cookies

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device. We use a small number of cookies:

Essential cookies

Used for basic site functionality (e.g. remembering you've dismissed a notice). No way to opt out of these without breaking the site, but they don't track you across the web.

Analytics cookies

We use a privacy-conscious analytics tool to count visits and understand usage patterns. The data is aggregated — we can't tell what you specifically did, only what visitors in aggregate did.

Advertising cookies

The site is supported by display ads. Ad networks (including Google AdSense and its partners) place cookies to show relevant ads. See section 5 below.

Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies via your browser's settings. Doing so won't break the calculators (they don't depend on cookies), but parts of the site might behave oddly.

5. Advertising

We use Google AdSense and possibly other ad networks to serve display advertising. These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites
  • Limit how many times you see the same ad
  • Measure ad effectiveness

Opting out of personalised ads:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings to opt out of Google's personalised advertising
  • Visit AboutAds.info to opt out across multiple ad networks
  • Visit Your Online Choices (Australia) for Australia-specific opt-outs

You'll still see ads if you opt out — they just won't be personalised.

6. Third-party services

We rely on a small set of trusted services to run the site:

  • Hosting — Cloud hosting providers (AWS, Cloudflare, or similar). Logs are governed by their privacy policies.
  • Google AdSense / ad partners — for serving ads (see section 5)
  • Analytics provider — to count visits (currently Google Analytics or a privacy-focused alternative)

Where we link to external sites (universities, UAC, VTAC, etc.), those sites have their own privacy policies. We're not responsible for their practices.

7. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you (if any)
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete information (where applicable)
  • Object to certain processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC)

To exercise these rights, email atarcalculators@gmail.com with your request. We respond within 30 days.

8. International visitors

The site is primarily for Australian residents, but accessible from anywhere. If you're visiting from outside Australia:

  • EU / UK visitors: we honour GDPR-style requests as best we can given our minimal data collection. The legal basis for processing is our legitimate interest in operating the site.
  • US visitors: we comply with applicable state privacy laws (CCPA, etc.) on request.
  • Other visitors: Australian Privacy Principles apply by default.

9. Children's privacy

The site is intended for Year 12 students, their parents, and educators — generally users aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we'll delete it.

10. Data security

We take reasonable steps to secure the limited data we do collect:

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Server access is restricted and password-protected
  • Calculator code runs entirely client-side (your marks never leave your browser)

No system is 100% secure. We can't guarantee absolute protection, but we work to minimise risks.

11. Data retention

  • Email correspondence: kept up to 24 months unless needed longer for ongoing matters
  • Server logs: kept up to 90 days for security and debugging
  • Analytics: retained according to our analytics provider's defaults (typically 14–26 months)
  • Marks you enter into calculators: never retained — they exist only in your browser session

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top
  • For significant changes, we'll add a visible notice on the homepage for at least 30 days

The current version always lives at this URL.

13. Contact us

Questions, concerns, or privacy requests:

  • General privacy questions: atarcalculators@gmail.com
  • Anything else: atarcalculators@gmail.com

This privacy policy is provided in good faith and reflects our genuine practices. It is not a substitute for legal advice. If you have legal concerns about how a website handles your data, consult a qualified lawyer.

For more on how the site works, see how we produce estimates on our methodology page, what you can and cannot rely on in our disclaimer, and the rules for using the site in our terms of use. Questions about your data can go to our contact page, and the about page explains who we are and why the site is free.
